About This Viz

I like this particular Visualization because it shows how Flags look much more alike when leaving out layouts and simply visualize them them as stacked bar charts according to color proportion.


It also reminds me of how much alike we all are if we just ignore the details. We can even see how blue, red and white are the most common colors in flags across the world.
